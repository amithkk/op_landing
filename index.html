Why Competitive Coding?

The question that we get asked the most is probably "Why bother learning competitive coding?" or "Where will this help me in real life?". Here's your answer!

01

Write Better Code

Constantly having to find innovative and often non-obvious ways to solve problems helps you write faster code

02

Solve Complex Problems

Knowing how to segment large problems as byte sized pieces will help you breakdown many complex computing problems

03

Get Placed!

Most software development companies have at least 1 interview round that is competitive coding

04

Compete and Get Recognised

Many CC competitions happen throughout the year like ICPC, ICO, CodeChef Challenges that you can win prizes and opportunities in
